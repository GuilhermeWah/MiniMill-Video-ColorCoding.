MiniMill Ball Detection System

Advanced computer vision application for detecting and tracking balls of varying sizes (4mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm) in mill processing videos. Perfect for enhancing presentations and highlighting key points during talks.

Start Processing Learn More

Key Features

Powerful detection capabilities designed for MiniMill presentations

Multi-Size Detection

Detect balls of 4mm, 6mm, 8mm, and 10mm sizes with precision, even with motion blur at speeds up to 75 RPM.

Color-Coded Overlays

Visual tracking with semi-transparent overlays: Blue (4mm), Green (6mm), Red (8mm), Orange (10mm).

Toggle Controls

Independent detection pipelines for each size. Toggle visibility on or off without reprocessing the entire video.

Real-Time Processing

Process videos at approximately 30 FPS, optimized for CPU-only environments. No high-end GPU required.
